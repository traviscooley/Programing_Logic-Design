Pet Finder

A simple pet finder program that ask a series of quetions to determine what your pet is that you picked.

  1. Prompt the use to pick a pet
  2. Pick a Pet
    • Hamster
    • Rabbit
    • Dog
    • Cat
    • Snake
    • Fish
    • Spider
    • Parrot
  3. Ask a series of quetions to determine what the pet that they picked is
  4. First split the animals into two catergories
  • Is your pet a Mammal (True)
    1. [Hamster, Rabbit, Dog, Cat]
      • Now we split it even further into two groups again.
      • Does your pet have big buck teeth? (True)
        1. [Hamster, Rabbit]
          • Finally we split the remaining two up and make a final decision on what their pet is.
          • Does your pet have long ears? (True)
            1. Your pet is a Rabbit
          • Does your pet not have long ears? (False)
            1. Your pet is a Hamster
      • Does your pet not have big buck teeth? (False)
        1. [Dog, Cat]
          • Finally we split the remaining two up and make a final decision on what their pet is.
          • Is your pet mans best friend? (True)
            1. Your pet is a Dog
          • Is your pet not mans best friend? (False)
            1. Your pet is a Cat
  • Is your pet not a Mammal (False)
    1. [Snake, Fish, Spider, Parrot]
      • Now we split it even further into two groups again.
      • Does your pet have scales? (True)
        1. [Snake, Fish]
          • Finally we split the remaining two up and make a final decision on what their pet is.
          • Does your pet eat rodents? (True)
            1. Your pet is a Snake
          • Does your pet not eat rodent? (False)
            1. Your pet is a Fish
      • Does your pet not have scales? (False)
        1. [Spider, Parrot]
          • Finally we split the remaining two up and make a final decision on what their pet is.
          • Does your pet have more than two legs? (True)
            1. Your pet is a Spider
          • Does your pet not have more than two legs? (False)
            1. Your pet is a Parrot

Choose a pet and we will find out what it is!
